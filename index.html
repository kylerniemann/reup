Reup

Privacy policy and product overview

Reup is a Chrome extension that notices visible reset-time messages on supported AI tools, saves those times locally, and alerts you when access should be coming back.

What Reup reads Visible page text and supported URLs needed to detect reset-related messages.
What Reup stores Saved reset times and your alert preferences in Chrome local storage.
What you control Whether Reup shows a visual banner, plays a chime, sends both, or stays quiet.

Privacy policy

Effective date: April 16, 2026

  • Reup uses visible page content only to detect reset-related messages on supported sites.
  • Reup stores detected reset times and your settings locally in the browser.
  • Reup does not sell user data and does not transfer user data to third parties for advertising or unrelated purposes.
  • Reup does not require an account or send your saved reset times to an external service.

Supported sites
